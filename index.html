About Marajuana

Pain relief is a common reason people use medical marijuana. The National Academies of Sciences, Engineering, and Medicine concluded in 2017 that good evidence exists to support this practice.4 Marijuana products also appear to be effective at calming muscle spasms caused by multiple sclerosis and easing nausea and vomiting due to chemotherapy, the report stated. The National Academies also determined that there is moderate evidence that cannabis or cannabis-derived products may help people who have trouble sleeping due to sleep apnea, fibromyalgia, or chronic pain.











DIFFERENT STRAINS

SATIVA

KUSHLATTO

Kushlato is an Indica-dom cross of Gelato x Kush Mints, bred by our good friends at Seed Junky Genetics. Limonene, Caryophyllene, and Linalool as its dominant terpenes, Kushlato has a complex flavor profile of spicy, citrus pine with subtle notes of cream, cinnamon, and lavender. This cultivar is a solid choice for sufferers of depression, anxiety, and inflammation.

MARSHMELLOW OG

Marshmellow is Known for its dominant terpenes, it has an earthy, pine, skunk flavor with hints of vanilla. Hard-hitting, without being overly sedative, this cultivar is well-suited for sufferers of depression, anxiety, and inflammation. Our cut was bred by Bay Area breeder Compound Genetics.

SUNSETFRITTERS

Sunset Fritter is an Indica dom cross of Apple Fritter x Sunset Sherbet. Featuring Limonene, Caryophyllene, and Myrcene as its dominant terpenes, it tastes like an apple-orange pie and offers euphoric, anti-inflammatory, and anti-anxiety effects.



HYBRID

SKYWALKER OG

An Indica-dominant hybrid cross of Mazar, Blueberry, and OG Kush, this cultivar comes from a clone originally bred by Darkheart Nursery with genetics from Dutch Passion Seeds out of Amsterdam. Skywalker OG strain has notes of sweet, citrus hops, and a spicy peppery finish. It offers mellow, euphoric effects, and heightened awareness and creativity. Perfect for a mellow, creative afternoon or playing video games.


AK-47

Don't let its intense name fool you: AK-47 will leave you relaxed and mellow. The result is a complex blend of flavors and effects. AK-47 may deliver a steady and long-lasting cerebral buzz that will keep you mentally alert and engaged in creative or social activities. While its scent is sour and earthy, its sweet, floral notes can only be fully realized in the taste.


MAC-1

MAC #1 or “the MAC”, also known as Miracle Alien Cookies, is an Indica-dominant hybrid cross of Alien Cookies x Colombian x Starfighter and grown from a clone cut bred by Dark Heart Nursery in San Leandro, California. With its heirloom Colombian Sativa genetics that are increasingly harder to find these days, balanced by the Indica-dominant Alien Cookies and Starfighter cultivars, MAC1 strain has incredible trichome production and long-lasting effects.
